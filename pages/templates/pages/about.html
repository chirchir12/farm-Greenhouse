About Us

Finetouch Africa Ltd has been in operation since 2010 providing its clients with value added services right from design, survey and construction of the relevant structures with customer focused business model. Finetouch Africa Ltd has undoubtedly risen steadily over a short period of time to become one of the leading service providers in greenhouse and irrigation construction in Kenya.

We are happy with the success of our customers across Kenya and beyond and generally the success of agribusiness sector. This emphasizes our business strategy which is focused at promoting the use of modern technologies in enhancing agricultural water and irrigation resource exploitation while placing value on customer satisfaction and service delivery.
